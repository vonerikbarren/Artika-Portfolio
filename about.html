Hello! I'm Erika Barren-Tucker the Artist of this website!

I am a graduate Alumni of DePaul University with a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Animation. I specialize in Tradtional and Digital Art. I am art student who dreams of doing my own animation for either my own personal success or do conceptual art in the film or game industry. I am also Autistic. I find my disability to be a strength rather then a weakness and I wish to prove that to the world. â€‹

For as long as I remembered I always loved to draw on paper. I often find myself very inspired by the world around me and often just draw it on a piece of paper. What started as a single paper grew to about hundreds as I continue to draw more and more until I decided to create my own original art pieces and characters. Story-telling, Animation and Character Design has always caught my interest at heart so I've spent most of my time, drawing characters, emotions, movement and continue to create new stories. It gives me a sense of wonder, adventure, excitement and emotions.

Now that I am out on my own, it is time to start my own career path as an Artist and Animator.
